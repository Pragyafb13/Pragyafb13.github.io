Arduino - BT Module

A simple circuit made with Arduino Uno Board, BT Module HC05 , few LED bulbs, connecting wires and an Android to control the lights. "Promeck" For Parkinsons

Our project, Promeck is a smart device which contributes to solving the issue of tremors for PD patients by assisting them with regular treatment. It helps the physician to provide physiotherapy treatment to the patients directly from the ease of their home or clinic.The device will be equally effective for the patients undergoing physiotherapy treatment. The project is still in development phase, we are researching on the network part for providing long-range connectivity.
